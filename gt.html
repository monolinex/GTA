THE STORY OF GAY TONY

Luis Lopez - a former street gangster turned loyal bodyguard - navigates the glittering underworld of Liberty City's nightlife alongside the flamboyant nightclub impresario Gay Tony. When Tony's empire starts crumbling under debts and threats from loan sharks, Luis must use his street smarts and combat skills to protect his boss and their business. Manage elite venues like Maisonette 9, deal with shady characters from the underworld, and survive the explosive collapse of Tony's nightclub empire in this high-octane expansion to GTA IV.
